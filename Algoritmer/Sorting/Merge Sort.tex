Split A ind i to halvdele, sorter disse to rekursivt, sammensæt de to undersæt (divide and conquer)\\
\textbf{Pseudokode:}
\begin{lstlisting}[frame=single, mathescape=true]
MergeSort(A, i, j)
	if i < j
		m = $\lfloor$(i+j)/2$\rfloor$
		MergeSort(A, i, m)
		MergeSort(A, m+1, j)
		Merge(A, i, m, j)
\end{lstlisting}
\textbf{Running time:} O(n log n)
